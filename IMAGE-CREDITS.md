during the build asset step (see `.tmp-img/watermark.mjs`). Originals are resized to
1200×800, optimised, and stored in `src/assets/articles/`.

## Adding more images (keep it AdSense-safe)

1. Source only from **Pexels**, **Unsplash**, or **Pixabay** (all free for commercial use),
   or use your own photos.
2. **Never** save images from Google Image search or other sites — most are copyrighted and
   will risk AdSense rejection and legal claims.
3. Run them through the watermark step, save to `src/assets/articles/`, reference via the
   `cover:` frontmatter field, and add a row to this table for proof.
